The Lion in the Zoo.

Once there was a lion in the zoo. The lion wanted to go out the zoo. But he was in a cage.

One day the cage got broken but the lion was fat and he couldn't get out.

The next morning the zoo keeper had to clean the cage and he left the door opened and the lion came out and he started to run and the people got scared.

Then the zoo keeper got him but the lion was strong. Then the lion stopped running and he roarrred. Then the lion went back to the cage

And everybody was happy
